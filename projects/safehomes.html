    About the Website

    The Safe Homes website is a comprehensive real estate platform designed to showcase properties available for sale or rent in Hyderabad. It provides a seamless experience for potential homebuyers to explore various types of properties, including residential and commercial spaces, across different locations in the city. The website highlights the services offered by Safe Homes, allowing users to learn more about the company’s expertise in property consultation, legal assistance, and home loan support.


    The platform features a dedicated section for client testimonials, where satisfied customers share their experiences, adding credibility and trust to the company’s reputation. Visitors interested in purchasing a property can easily get in touch with Safe Homes through a contact form integrated directly on the website. This form allows potential buyers to inquire about specific properties, schedule viewings, or request additional details. With a focus on user convenience, the website ensures that clients can browse and filter properties, view detailed descriptions and images, and contact the company with ease.


    I developed a fully responsive portfolio website using HTML, CSS, JS, PHP and Bootstrap to showcase my projects, skills, and resume. The website is designed to provide a clean, professional, and user-friendly interface. Check out the live website here: Live SHomes Website.

    Technologies Used

    This Website was developed using a combination of modern web technologies to ensure a seamless, responsive, and well-structured user experience:

    • HTML5: Structured the website's content, including service sections, property listings, testimonials, and the contact form.
    • CSS3: Used for custom styling to create a professional, clean layout, with a responsive design to ensure the website works seamlessly across various devices, from desktops to smartphones.
    • JavaScript: Enabled interactive elements, such as property filters, form validation, and dynamic content loading for property details and testimonials.
    • Bootstrap: Implemented to provide a responsive grid system, making the layout adaptable to different screen sizes. Bootstrap components like the navbar, cards, and forms were used to enhance the UI while maintaining consistency across pages.
    • PHP: Utilized to process the contact form, allowing potential buyers to directly connect with the company for inquiries or property visits.
